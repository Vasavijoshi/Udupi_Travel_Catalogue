Welcome to Udupi

It's time to start your adventure

Udupi district, along Karnataka’s coast, offers pristine beaches, spiritual heritage, adventure, and rich traditions. Relax on Malpe and Kapu Beaches or dive into scuba diving, parasailing, and jet skiing.

Seek blessings at Sri Krishna Temple, Kollur Mookambika and more, each steeped in history. Experience the mystical Bhoota Kola, where folklore comes to life. Nature lovers can escape to Agumbe’s misty peaks, trek Kudlu Falls, or unwind at a Chikkamagaluru resort. With a perfect mix of tradition, adventure, and stunning landscapes, it offers unforgettable journey.
